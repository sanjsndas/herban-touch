Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Herban Touch Pruning Services, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services, including personal information like your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and any other information you provide when using our website or contacting us. We also collect technical data such as IP addresses, browser type, and browsing behavior through cookies and similar technologies.

3. Use of Personal Information

Your personal information helps us to communicate effectively, deliver our services, process payments, and offer customer support. Additionally, we use the data to personalize your experience, improve our website, and send newsletters or marketing messages as permitted by you. We do not sell your information to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data in accordance with applicable laws, including your consent, fulfillment of contracts, compliance with legal obligations, protection of vital interests, and our legitimate interests to enhance and secure our services. We ensure your rights are respected throughout our processing activities.

5. Data Collection Methods

The information is collected directly from you when you enter data via forms, communicate with us, or use our services. We also collect information automatically through website analytics and tracking technologies like cookies, pixel tags, and server logs to understand website usage and improve performance.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device to enhance your browsing experience. We use cookies to remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and monitor website performance. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with unrelated third parties. However, we may share data with service providers and partners who assist us in operating our business, providing services, or compliance with the law. All such parties are obligated to maintain confidentiality and data protection standards.

8. Data Security

Protecting your personal data is a priority. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits, to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data stored by us.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it to prevent identification.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to processing, and data portability. You can exercise these rights by contacting us, and we will respond within the regulatory timeframes.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for your convenience. We do not control these external sites and are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit through our links.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we collected data from a child without parental consent, we will delete it promptly. Parents should monitor their children’s internet activity.

13. International Data Transfers

Data collected may be processed or stored in countries other than your own. We ensure that any transfer of your personal data complies with applicable data protection laws and that adequate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or certifications.

14. Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send you promotional emails or newsletters about our services, offers, or industry updates. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link or contacting us directly. We respect your marketing preferences and will not spam your inbox.

15. Data Accuracy

We strive to keep your personal information accurate and up-to-date. Please notify us promptly if your details change or if you believe we maintain inaccurate information about you, so we can correct or update our records accordingly.

16. User Accounts

If you create an account on our website, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials. You agree to notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account, and we are not liable for any loss or damage arising from your failure to keep your account secure.

17. Security Breach Notification

In case of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law. We are committed to transparency and will keep you informed about the nature and impact of any incident.

18. Cookie Consent

We seek your consent for the use of non-essential cookies through banners or pop-ups when you visit our website. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or our website tools to enhance your browsing experience while respecting your privacy.

19. Data Controller Contact

If you have questions or concerns about our data processing practices, you can contact our Data Protection Officer or customer service by the contact details provided at the bottom of this page. We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns promptly and thoroughly.

20. Use of Analytics Services

We use web analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website. These tools collect data anonymously or pseudonymously to help us improve the user experience and measure effectiveness. We ensure the data collected is handled securely and used only for legitimate purposes.

21. Data Minimization

We collect only the necessary personal data to provide our services effectively. Unnecessary or excessive data collection is avoided to protect your privacy, in line with current data protection regulations.

22. User-Generated Content

If you provide feedback, reviews, or other content on our website, you grant us permission to use, display, and distribute this content for marketing and promotional purposes while respecting your privacy preferences.

23. Privacy Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant updates through our website or other means. Continued use of our services implies acceptance of the updated policy.

24. Marketing Preferences Management

You can manage your communication preferences and opt out of marketing messages at any time by contacting us or using links included in emails. We respect your choices and will promptly remove you from mailing lists as requested.

25. Data Access and Portability

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to receive it in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. This enables you to transfer data to another service provider if desired.

26. Data Correction and Deletion

You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data or request deletion where there is no legal reason for continued storage. We will respond to such requests in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

27. Data Processing Agreement

We ensure that all third parties involved in data processing adhere to strict data protection agreements, ensuring your personal information is handled securely and in compliance with privacy laws.

28. Marketing Analytics and Segmentation

We may use aggregated data for marketing analytics and customer segmentation to improve our services and offerings. This data is anonymized and does not identify individual users.

29. Secure Payment Processing

When you make payments, sensitive information such as credit card numbers is processed securely through trusted payment gateways and not stored by us. We employ encryption and security protocols to protect payment data.

30. Email Security

We take measures to protect information transmitted to us via email but advise users to avoid sending sensitive information by email due to inherent security risks. Contact us through secure forms where possible.

31. Social Media Integration

Our website may integrate with social media platforms to facilitate sharing and interaction. These platforms have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them when sharing content through such services.

32. Data Breach Procedures

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we have procedures in place to detect, respond, and notify affected individuals and authorities promptly as required by law to minimize potential harm.

33. Anonymized Data Use

We may collect and utilize anonymized or aggregated data for business analysis, performance improvement, and research purposes. Such data cannot be traced back to any individual.

34. Geographic Data

With your permission, we may collect geographic data to provide location-based services or improvements. This data is handled with care and only used for intended purposes.

35. Third-Party Advertising

We do not engage in third-party behavioral advertising or share personal data with advertisers. Our marketing efforts are managed directly by our team respecting your privacy.

36. Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of receiving marketing communications and certain data processing activities at any time without affecting your use of our core services. We provide clear mechanisms for doing so in all our communications and on our website.

37. Data Protection Officer

We have appointed a Data Protection Officer responsible for overseeing data privacy compliance and addressing any questions or concerns you may have about our handling of your personal information.

38. Complaints and Disputes

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you have the right to file complaints with appropriate data protection authorities. We encourage you to contact us first to resolve any issues amicably.

39. International Users

If you are accessing our services from outside the country where we operate, please be aware that your data will be transferred, processed, and stored under our privacy practices, which may differ from those in your location.
